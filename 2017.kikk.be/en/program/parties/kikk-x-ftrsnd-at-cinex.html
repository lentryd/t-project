Line up

Kangding Ray, Bétøn, 2559 & Hawkan b2b Polarized

The closing party of KIKK Festival will be organized in collaboration with FTRSND, the Brussels' based collective behind Brussels Electronic Marathon. We have prepared a showcase of the best Brussels' based electronic music scene along with the famous Kangding Ray.

KANGDING RAY (live) - Soundcloud

Kangding ray's music reflects his wide musical background, and a particular way of merging beats and atmospheric soundscapes into slowly evolving compositions. KR combines machines with « real instruments » in a subtle and rythmic suite, integrating noises, walls of distortion, massive bass lines, voices and field recordings, blending techno grooves with dark textures, always on the edge between experimental music and club culture. 

BÉTØN (live) - Link

Bétøn is a modular music project. Its members are neither fixed nor defined and all come from FTRSND collective.

These musicians perform live, mixing together all kind of audio materials. Using loops, drum machines and synthesizers they create a unique performance following the direction given by both the musicians and the audience’s energy.
Bétøn’s artists have already performed in places like Ancienne Belqique, Botanique, Dour, Fuse, MIMA, Recyclart, TEDx Bozar , Flagey Studio 4, amongst others and shared stage with artists like Kangding Ray, Claudio PRC, Abdulla Rashim, Cio d’Or, Artefakt, Iori, Fennesz...

Stage design realized by SOPHIE LE MEILLOUR Website 

Sophie Le Meillour is a media artist based in Geneva. In 2011, she graduated in graphic design and video from the HEAD (Haute Ecole d’Art et de Design) in Switzerland. She is composing her own raw material from traditional disciplines to deploy a sensitive storytelling through live compositions and performances. Since 2009, she has been exploring the multiple facets of the image, from the line and movement to new media and film footage. Through her drawings, photographs, videos and installations, she is developing her own immersive and dreamlike work which she shares with the musical and visual universes of fellow artists. Materials and textures are at the core of her experiments. However, she manhandles them in her very own peculiar way throughout her different projects.
