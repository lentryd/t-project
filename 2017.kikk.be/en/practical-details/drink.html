Place du Vieux (aka Place du Marché aux Légumes)

4 min. by foot

A square with a handful of bars, the perfect place to relax with a fresh belgian beer after an intense day of conferences. One of the local's favourite spots for going out. Officially named "Place du Marché aux Légumes", La Place du Vieux is also the KIKK unofficial HQ for late evening drinks.

Le Piano Bar

Le Piano Bar

4 min. by foot

Bar located on the Place du Vieux. The meeting place for those who are already in Namur on Wednesday. It’s also the venue for the KIKK afterparty on Friday 4 November, 2016. Chill atmosphere, good music and often live music on Saturday night. A good choice for a nice evening!

Les Poules à Lier

4 min. by foot

Just next to Piano bar, it's another bar where to spend a good evening. By the way, it's also the bar for the KIKK after party on Thursday 3 November. After the party at Théâtre de Namur, the survivors will be warmly welcomed at Poules à Lier, there will be a special cocktail for the KIKK participants.

Vino Vino

Vino Vino

5 min. by foot

For wine lovers (as you can tell by the name), a place to try out some wines and hear about their story, their region and their taste. The passionate owner of this wine bar is and captivating. He has a wide selection that he regularly renew. For hungry folks, you can taste one of the delicious appetisers, often typical from the region.

Café du Collège

5 min. by foot

One of the last bistros of Namur, masters at serving beers. And the master in question is quite a character! A typical belgian bar where you'll get mingle with the locals.

Bouffon du Roi

Bouffon du Roi

6 min. by foot

From the outside it doesn't look special but once inside you will not want to leave. A nice bar for spending an evening with friends: dark lit atmosphere, comfy sofas, beer of the month on the tap, board games, football table and a petanque field in the back courtyard for the sunny days!

Le Merle

Le Merle

8 min. by foot

On the menu: a wide range of belgian beers that will make you go crazy! Here it's forbidden to order soft drinks or a simple blond beer; you have to try special beers! If the list seems endless the owner will be happy to advise you. The small wooden rustic tables give the tone of a warm and cosy atmosphere. One or two special beers might give you the guts to go on stage to play a few notes on the piano. Jams and concerts are regularly organised there. Be careful this bar is only open from 4 pm and is closed on weekends.
