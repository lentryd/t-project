Le Temps des Cerises

Le Temps des Cerises

+/- 20 €
3 min. by foot

A typical "cuisine du terroir" restaurant, a tiny place with a lovely warm atmosphere which is definitely a contrast from the neighborhood's brasseries. Sit down and look around, discover the tribute to the local celebrities and other curiosities proudly exhibited by the owner. Sweetbread with morels or oven roasted knuckle of ham grated with mustard sauce, let yourself be tempted by a tasty traditional dish. It's a tiny cosy place with only a few tables so be sure to book in advance!

Ogawa

Ogawa

+/- 12 €
3 min. by foot

This japanese restaurant is the best in Namur proposing extra fresh sushis, exquisite sashimis and surprising tempuras. Not so fond of raw fish? Don't worry, you'll also find yummy hot dishes on the menu! Ogawa's decoration is pretty sober and the atmosphere is... well, japanese! For more genuineness, eat alone at the counter. Try out the sparkling saké for finishing your meal on a funky note!

Tasty Bar & Burger

Tasty Bar & Burger

+/- 15 €
3 min. by foot

At Tasty treat yourself with a good burger that will satisfy your hunger until the next day! Here the meat is local, potatoes are from the village next door, the buns are also from the hood. If you can't wait to eat, order some onion rings or nachos along with the house's aperitif because the waiting time is often a bit long. For a classic burger, go for the Tasty, as its name says it's pretty good! If you're more adventurous you'll definitely have to try "Le Moine" with its abbey cheese.

Emocion

Emocion

+/- 15 €
5 min. by foot

Planchas, bruschettas, tapas, pastas,... The choice is difficult in this small mediterranean restaurant of the pedestrian area but all the dishes are worth it. Pépite - Cave à Manger

Ot's Burger

Ot's Burger

+/- 5 €
3 min. by foot

This local fast food is a true institution in Namur! Thanks to their quality burgers and special belgian sauces, Ot's managed to survive and compete against the large fast food chains! La Petite Boulangerie
